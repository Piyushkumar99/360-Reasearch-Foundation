Agriculture

The Department of Agriculture (DA) at 360 Research Foundation works to develop plans and policies for the development of agriculture, animal husbandry, dairy, fisheries, agricultural land policies and food processing for famers’ welfare. It works in close coordination with the agriculture departments of national and state partners as well as regional.
